About Us

Company Sathi specializes in simplifying company registration and legal documentation, backed by over 20 years of industry experience. Our team of seasoned experts is dedicated to guiding you through every step of the registration process, ensuring a seamless and efficient experience. Whether you are looking to establish a Private Limited Company, Public Limited Company, or a Non-profit Organization under the Companies Act 2063, we provide comprehensive support tailored to your specific needs. Operating across Nepal, we help you navigate the complexities of legal requirements, ensuring your business is registered or incorporated under your desired name with full compliance and precision.
